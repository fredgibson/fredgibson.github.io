We want to make you smile again.
Tell us about your concern and we’ll do our best to help.

We care about giving our Members a positive experience each and every time they visit us. 

We're serious about Member satisfaction and committed to resolving any issue about the service you received. Following steps 1 to 3 is the fastest way to help us help you. 

Step 1: The best way to begin is to raise the issue with the Meridian staff member where your concern originated. 

Step 2: If it isn't resolved, ask to have it escalated to the Branch Manager.

Step 3: If you're not satisfied with the resolution(s) provided in step 1 and/or 2, escalate your concern in writing to the Member Relations Office by selecting 'TELL US ABOUT IT'
